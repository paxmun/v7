Justicia En La Conferencia

PAXMUN’17
Pieces to Peace
August 10-11

With great gusto and inspiration DPS Greater Noida hosts the 4th edition of its annual Model United Nations, PAXMUN’17, to rekindle its core agenda of attaining PAX, unite the world through PEACE. It is an attempt to enable disjoint boundaries to come together, talk out their differences, work for the betterment of humanity and emerge as a byword of peace - from ‘Pieces to Peace’. Budding think tanks are hereby heartily invited to deliberate, unite and solve contemporary- economic, social and environmental issues. Come, join us and speak your mind in the two day PAXMUN’17 Conference.

Let’s help each other with luminous ideas to make the world a Peaceful, Prosperous and Propitious place to live in.
